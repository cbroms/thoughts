- 1 cup flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- ¾ tsp baking powder
- ¾ tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt 
- 1¼ cups buttermilk
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp butter, melted

Mix the dry ingredients, then mix in the wet ingredients. Make pancakes.
