- ½ cup unsalted butter
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg
- 1 cup flour
- ½ tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup chocolate chips

Cream the butter with the sugar. Add the egg and vanilla. Mix in the dry ingredients.  

Bake at 350℉ for 10min.
