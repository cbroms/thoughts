- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup flour 
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp oil

Mix. Use about ⅓ cup of the batter for one pancake, tipping the  pan to disperse. Cook at a medium-high sort of heat, they should brown on the first side in about 90 seconds or so.
